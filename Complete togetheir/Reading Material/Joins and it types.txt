# ğŸŒŸ **What Are JOINs in MySQL?**

A **JOIN** is used to combine rows from two or more tables **based on a related column** between them.

Think of it like matching data from two lists where one column connects them.

Example:

* Table **students** (student_id, name)
* Table **marks** (student_id, score)
  You use a JOIN to show each student *with* their marks.
